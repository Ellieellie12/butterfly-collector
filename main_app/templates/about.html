All About Being A Buttefly Collector

The best part of collecting butterflies is being able to let them back out into the wild after I study them. When I am out catching them it always feels like time doesn't exist. A very important tip if you decide to get into catching butterflies is that you have to be very careful not to hit their wings. Overall this has been such a life changing hobby of mine and I hope for you it becomes something you hold dear to your heart.
